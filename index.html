My Experience

I attended the University of Central Florida for my undergraduate studies and the University of Idaho for my Master's in Human Factors Psychology. I have over five years of research experience and five months for experience as an Ergonomics Intern (industrial and office). I have taught 3 semesters of public speaking to undergraduate students at the U of I. Before receiving my Master's degree I worked in an elementary school for children with special needs as a teacher's assistant.

I have skills in the Adobe Creative Suite, Eyetracking, Statistical Packages (such as R and SPSS), Prototyping, and User Testing.
